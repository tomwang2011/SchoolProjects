    RESEARCH INTERESTS:

    My main area of interest is applied computational mathematics, computer algebra, experimental mathematics, and pen-based computing. Other interests include discrete mathematics, analysis of algorithms and automated theorem proving. In area of computer algebra, I am mostly interested in integration, summation, simplification, factorization, and computation of various special functions.
