    Bio Sketch
    Sukanya Manna received her Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Australian National University, Australia in 2011 and MS in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Taiwan University, Taiwan in 2006. She has spent a year as a postdoctoral researcher at Texas State University, USA. In 2013, joined as an Assistant Professor in the department of Computer Science at California State Polytechnic University Pomona, USA. She has been awarded different prestigious scholarships to pursue her studies. Her research includes several related fields including Text mining, Information Retrieval, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.
